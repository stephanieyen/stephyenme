Welcome. I work in tech startups, write about what matters, and create music that moves.

Composing and producing soundscapes that tell stories without words. From ambient textures to full arrangements.

Process: I compose primarily with piano and modular synths, layering textures and building narratives through sound. Each piece starts with a feeling I want to capture.
